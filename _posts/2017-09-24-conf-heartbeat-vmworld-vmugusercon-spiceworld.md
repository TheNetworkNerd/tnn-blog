### Are You Going to VMUG UserCon?

Not everyone can attend VMworld, but there are other conferences happening this year that can be of value.  Whether you are a VMware shop or not, a very valuable one day free conference is the [VMUG UserCon](https://www.vmug.com/Attend/VMUG-UserCon).  These are held across the world at different times during the year, and I recommend joining your local [VMUG](https://www.vmug.com/) to stay updated on conference dates and other free meetings throughout the year.

And if you happen to be somewhere near Dallas / Fort Worth, the [DFW UserCon](https://community.spiceworks.com/topic/2049541-if-you-re-close-to-dfw-and-can-t-go-to-spiceworld-go-to-dfw-vmug-usercon) is on October 10th, which is a nice free alternative for those not able to attend [Spiceworld Austin](https://www.spiceworks.com/spiceworld/agenda/).

### Spiceworld is Coming!  Do You have Your SpiceBuddy?

If you are headed to Spiceworld this year for the first time, check out [this thread](https://community.spiceworks.com/topic/2058722-spiceworld-newbie-fear-not-spicebuddies-are-here) to learn more about the SpiceBuddy program and all the fun events we have planned.  Keep an eye on the [Spiceworld forum](https://community.spiceworks.com/spiceworld) in the community for additional updates, and plan to come to one of the [Newbie Bootcamps](https://www.spiceworks.com/spiceworld/agenda/) on Monday, October 9th (5-6 PM and 6:30-7:30 PM sessions during the Sponsor Showcase).  There is no need to sign up.  Just show up!  


![](do-you-have-your-spicebuddy.jpg)  
 

When it's all over, [come have a cup of coffee with us](https://community.spiceworks.com/topic/2059327-the-post-spiceworld-coffee-shindig-is-back-courtesy-of-the-spicebuddy-program) to relive the experience before heading home.  Whether this is your first time or your fifth time to attend Spiceworld, I hope to see you there!
